# Security Policy for Library UNIDA Gontor

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly:

**Email:** security@unida.gontor.ac.id  
**Alternate:** library@unida.gontor.ac.id

## Response Time

- Initial response: 48 hours
- Status update: 7 days
- Resolution target: 30 days

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- library.unida.gontor.ac.id
- perpustakaan.unida.gontor.ac.id
- Associated APIs and services

## Preferred Languages

- Bahasa Indonesia
- English

## Public Key (PGP)

Contact us for our PGP public key.
